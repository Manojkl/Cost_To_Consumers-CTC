7. FeTaQA: Free-form table question answering
   a. Paper: https://scholar.google.com/scholar_url?url=https://direct.mit.edu/tacl/article-pdf/doi/10.1162/tacl_a_00446/2012156/tacl_a_00446.pdf&hl=en&sa=T&oi=gsr-r-gga&ct=res&cd=0&d=13900588696844028848&ei=QS0RZ_yJBLa56rQP1tOf0Qw&scisig=AFWwaeZSzYXXIx1YsRAna1uqDtbc 
   b. Github: https://github.com/Yale-LILY/FeTaQA
   c. Purpose: Evaluating a QA system’s ability to understand queries and retrieve short-form answers from
               tabular data and FeTaQA introduces elements that require deeper reasoning and integration of information. This
               includes generating free-form text answers that involve the retrieval, inference, and integration of multiple
               discontinuous facts from structured knowledge sources like tables. This requires the model to generate long,
               informative, and free-form answers.
